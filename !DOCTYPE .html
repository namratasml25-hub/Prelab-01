Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

About

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's missile and nuclear programs.

Achievements

He was awarded the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor. Known for his humility and dedication, he inspired millions of students.

Legacy

Dr. Kalam remains a symbol of knowledge, innovation, and leadership. His life continues to motivate young minds across the world.
